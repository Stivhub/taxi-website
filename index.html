Discover the beauty of the Golden City

Heritage

Jaisalmer Fort

Rising from the golden Thar sands, this UNESCO-listed living fort is one of the largest in the world, home to havelis, temples, and vibrant markets.

Book Now
Desert

Sam Sand Dunes

Experience magical sunsets over vast golden dunes. Enjoy camel safaris, desert camps, and traditional Rajasthani folk performances under the stars.

Book Now
Architecture

Patwon Ki Haveli

A stunning cluster of five grand havelis adorned with exquisite mirror-work, paintings, and the finest sandstone carvings in all of Rajasthan.

Book Now
Nature

Gadisar Lake

A tranquil oasis surrounded by ornate temples and ghats. Enjoy peaceful boat rides and witness migratory birds in the heart of the desert city.

Book Now
Mystery

Kuldhara Village

An abandoned 13th-century village wrapped in legend. Walk through its haunting sandstone ruins and uncover the mystery of an overnight exodus.

Book Now
Royal

Bada Bagh

A garden of royal cenotaphs honoring Rajput rulers, framed against breathtaking desert sunsets. A photographer's dream destination.

Book Now
Spiritual

Tanot Mata Temple

A sacred temple near the India-Pakistan border, known for its history during the 1965 war and strong spiritual significance.

Book Now
Historic

Longewala Border

Historic battlefield of the 1971 war, now a popular patriotic tourist site with tanks and army exhibits.

Book Now
Jain Heritage

Lodurva (Lodhruva)

An ancient Jain pilgrimage site known for its beautifully carved temples and historic significance, located just outside Jaisalmer.
